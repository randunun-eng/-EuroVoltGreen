About Eurovolt

Leading manufacturer of advanced solar electronics since 2020

Our Story

Eurovolt is a leading manufacturer of advanced solar inverters and charge controllers, based in Shenzhen, China - the heart of global electronics innovation.

Founded in 2020, we specialize in designing and manufacturing high-efficiency solar electronics for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our product line includes hybrid inverters, on-grid and off-grid inverters, MPPT charge controllers, and PWM charge controllers.

We partner with renowned global brands including NEXT, POWMr, SUMRy, EASUN POWER, MUST, and ANERN, providing OEM/ODM manufacturing services and custom solutions for the worldwide solar energy market.

Our Mission

Accelerate global renewable energy adoption by manufacturing the most advanced solar electronics - focusing on intelligent power management systems that exclude solar panels.

Our Vision

Positioning as the global leader in solar electronics manufacturing, recognized for breakthrough power conversion technology.
