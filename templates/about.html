Our Story

Eurovolt specializes in manufacturing advanced solar electronics and power conversion equipment - everything except solar panels. Since our founding, we've been dedicated to creating the intelligent systems that make solar energy efficient and reliable.

Based in Shenzhen, China's technology hub, we combine state-of-the-art manufacturing with intensive R&D to deliver precision-engineered solar inverters, MPPT charge controllers, and PWM charge controllers. Our advanced electronics optimize energy conversion, battery management, and grid integration.

Through strategic partnerships with world-renowned brands like NET, POWMr, SUMRy, EASUN, MUST, and ANERN, we continue to expand our global reach and technological capabilities.

Our Mission

To accelerate global renewable energy adoption by manufacturing the most advanced solar electronics and power management systems, excluding panels. We focus on the intelligent components that maximize energy efficiency and system reliability.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in solar electronics manufacturing, recognized for breakthrough power conversion technology, intelligent energy management systems, and innovation in everything solar except the panels themselves.
